This is an academic research project conducted through the University of Pennsylvania. In this game, evidence will appear on screen indicating the location of a source. You're job will be to determine if the evidence suggests the source is above or below the line in the center of the screen. There will be 12 blocks of trials. The task will take about 30 minutes to complete.

You must be at least 18 years old to participate. Your participation in this research is voluntary, which means you can choose whether or not to participate without adverse consequences. Your anonymity is assured: the researchers who have requested your participation will not receive any personal information about you except gender and age. The de-identified data may be stored and distributed for future research studies without additional informed consent.

If you have questions about this research, please contact Chris Pizzica by emailing cpizzica@pennmedicine.upenn.edu.
